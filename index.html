Biography

Austin Matthews is an American professional ice hockey center and alternate captain for the Toronto Maple Leafs of the National Hockey League. Born in San Ramon, California, Matthews and his family moved to Scottsdale, Arizona when he was an infant. Matthews became the first player in modern NHL history to score four goals in his NHL debut. He scored 40 goals in his first season in 2016‐17, setting the Maple Leafs rookie record and becoming just the second rookie since the 2004‐05 lockout to reach the milestone (along with Alexander Ovechkin); he is just the fourth teenager in league history to accomplish the feat.
